STUDY 001 / LEMNISCATE OF BERNOULLI

Mathematical Equation toArchitecture

Lemniscate of Bernoulli

X(t) = a * cos(t) / (1 + sin(t)^2)

y(t) = a * sin(t) * cos(t) / (1 + sin(t)^2)

T ranges over the interval [0, 2π)

The equation is evaluated across a defined parameter range, producing multiple numerical outputs rather than a single value. These outputs are assigned to the X, Y, and Z axes to generate points in three-dimensional space. The points are reconstructed as a spatial curve, which becomes the geometric framework for the architectural form.
