01 / OVERVIEW

Project overview

Shila (Stone) Museum explores stone as both material and meaning through an architectural journey carved into a quarry. The project transforms the quarry itself into the exhibition, guiding visitors through descending and ascending paths that reveal the relationship between earth, time, and memory.
